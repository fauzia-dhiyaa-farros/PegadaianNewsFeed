## NewsFeed Portal System for PT Pegadaian Kanwil XI
This project aims to develop a centralized Newsfeed Portal System for PT Pegadaian Regional Office XI (Kantor Wilayah XI). The system is designed to distribute internal information, corporate announcements, performance updates, employee achievements, CSR activities, and operational policies in a structured and accessible digital platform.
## Expected Impact
### Short-Term Benefits
- Faster distribution of official announcements
- Reduced misinformation across branches
- Improved coordination between Kanwil and branch offices

### Long-Term Benefits
- Strengthened internal communication culture
- Increased employee engagement and transparency
- Digital transformation support for operational governance
- Centralized documentation of corporate activities
